About Me

Crafting Visual Narratives

I'm Christian Reyes, a freelance video editor with a passion that started back in junior high school. What began with editing school projects and friends' videos has grown into a professional career. I honed my skills through self-learning and taking on commission projects in senior high, exploring various editing styles and software like Premiere Pro, After Effects, and even 3D with Blender and Cinema 4D.

Currently, I am the video editor for the popular Pinoy YouTuber, PAOLUL, where I edit daily meme content, vlogs, and gaming videos, having completed over 200 videos for the channel. I thrive on bringing creative ideas to life and continuously push myself by joining photography and graphic design competitions.
